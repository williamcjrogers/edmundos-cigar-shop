Our Story

Located at 823 Fulham Road, near Chelsea, Edmundo's Cigar Shop is a traditional Havana Cigar Specialist that has earned official recognition for the depth and diversity of our Havana range.

A traditional establishment where gentlemen gather to enjoy the finest Cuban cigars. With classic outdoor seating to watch the world go by, and a carefully curated selection of premium accessories including Trufitt & Hill grooming products and luxury board games.

Led by Omar Khan, Edmundo's prides itself on offering the very best quality Cuban cigars—available by the box or individually. The collection is expertly stored in humidors crafted by Matthew Mason Carpentry.
